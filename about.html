About Talk2us

We Listen. We Care.

Who We Are

At Talk2us, we believe that every voice deserves to be heard. In today’s fast-paced world, people are often too busy to pause, reflect, and listen—not only to others but even to themselves. This is where we step in.

We dedicate ourselves to providing a safe, compassionate, and judgment-free space where individuals can share their thoughts, feelings, and struggles openly. Our trained professionals and empathetic volunteers walk beside you on your journey, helping you navigate life’s challenges, gain clarity, and rediscover strength within.

Dr. Lalit Mohan Pant
Founder - Talk2Us

Our Vision

To empower individuals of all ages in Haldwani and beyond to discover their true potential, embrace their uniqueness, and lead more meaningful and fulfilled lives.

Our Mission

  • Offer guidance and clarity in moments of confusion.
  • Provide non-judgmental support during times of emotional stress.
  • Encourage self-growth and healing by empowering people with tools to face challenges.
  • Build a community of trust and care where every individual feels valued.

Our Approach

We combine professional counseling techniques with empathetic listening. Whether you’re dealing with stress, career dilemmas, family issues, or emotional challenges, our approach is centered on:

  • Active Listening: Truly hearing what you have to say.
  • Guided Reflection: Helping you see your thoughts and feelings more clearly.
  • Personalized Support: Offering solutions tailored to your situation.
  • Community Engagement: Hosting awareness programs, workshops, and seminars to reach more people.

Our Team

Behind Talk2us is a dedicated group of counselors, psychologists, and support staff who share one goal—to make mental health care accessible and stigma-free.

  • Counselors & Psychologists: Trained professionals who provide one-on-one guidance.
  • Volunteers: Individuals passionate about listening and helping others.
  • Support Partners: Collaborators who help us reach more communities.
